# Assign a Contact to a General Contractor Profile

Use this workflow when a general contractor profile needs a linked contact. This keeps the GC record connected to the right person for communication, project references, and directory lookup.

## Open General Contractors

Navigate to **Directory > General Contractors**.

![General Contractors list](/help/scribe/assign-contact-to-general-contractor-profile/scribe-assets/step-01.png)

Select the general contractor profile you need to update.

![General contractor selected](/help/scribe/assign-contact-to-general-contractor-profile/scribe-assets/step-02.png)

## Edit the Profile

Select **Edit Profile** from the general contractor detail page.

![Edit Profile action](/help/scribe/assign-contact-to-general-contractor-profile/scribe-assets/step-03.png)

## Find the Contact

In the contacts section, click **Search contacts...**.

![Contact search field](/help/scribe/assign-contact-to-general-contractor-profile/scribe-assets/step-04.png)

Type the contact name or search term.

Choose the correct contact from the results.

![Contact result selected](/help/scribe/assign-contact-to-general-contractor-profile/scribe-assets/step-06.png)

## Save the Profile

Select **Save Changes** to update the general contractor profile.

![Save Changes action](/help/scribe/assign-contact-to-general-contractor-profile/scribe-assets/step-07.png)

After saving, confirm the contact appears on the general contractor profile.

![Linked contact on general contractor profile](/help/scribe/assign-contact-to-general-contractor-profile/scribe-assets/step-08.png)

## Quick Checklist

- The selected profile is the correct general contractor.
- The contact search result matches the person you want to link.
- **Save Changes** is selected after adding the contact.
- The contact appears on the profile after saving.
