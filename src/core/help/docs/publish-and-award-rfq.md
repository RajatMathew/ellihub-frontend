# Publish and Award an RFQ

Use this guide after an RFQ has been created and is ready to move through vendor invitation, quote review, vendor communication, and award. The screenshots show sample vendors, amounts, and email details only; use the actual vendor list, quote values, lead times, documents, and award amount for the project.

## Publish and Invite Vendors

Open the draft RFQ and select **Publish**.

![Draft RFQ Publish action](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-02.jpeg)

Open the vendor selector.

![Vendor selector](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-03.jpeg)

Search for and choose the vendors that should receive the RFQ.

![Vendor selected](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-05.jpeg)

Review the selected vendor contact information.

![Vendor contact selected](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-06.jpeg)

Select **Publish RFQ** after the vendor list is ready.

![Publish RFQ action](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-07.jpeg)

Confirm with **Publish & Invite**.

![Publish and Invite confirmation](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-08.jpeg)

## Record Vendor Quotes

Open the vendor actions menu for the vendor quote that needs to be recorded.

![Vendor actions menu](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-09.jpeg)

Select **Upload Quote**.

![Upload Quote action](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-10.jpeg)

Enter the quoted amount, lead time, any adjustment or discount, and attach the quote document when available.

![Quote amount field](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-12.jpeg)

![Quote lead time field](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-13.jpeg)

![Quote document upload](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-15.jpeg)

Select **Upload Quote** to save the vendor's quote.

![Upload vendor quote confirmation](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-16.jpeg)

Repeat the same process for additional vendor quotes.

![Second vendor quote action](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-18.jpeg)

![Second quote document upload](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-25.jpeg)

## Compare Quotes

After multiple quotes are recorded, select **Compare Quotes**.

![Compare quotes action](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-27.jpeg)

Review vendor quote rows and compare amount, lead time, and quoted status.

![First vendor quote comparison](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-28.jpeg)

![Second vendor quote comparison](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-29.jpeg)

## Email Vendors

Select **Email All Vendors** when the RFQ or quote information needs to be emailed to the vendor list.

![Email All Vendors action](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-31.jpeg)

Continue through the email preparation flow.

![Email preparation continue](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-33.jpeg)

Choose the sender account.

![Sender account selection](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-34.jpeg)

Review recipients, CC, BCC, subject, body, and generated RFQ PDF content before sending.

![Email review screen](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-35.jpeg)

![Email subject field](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-38.jpeg)

![Email body field](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-39.jpeg)

Select **Send RFQ Email**.

![Send RFQ Email action](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-41.jpeg)

Confirm the email sent message appears.

![Email sent confirmation](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-42.jpeg)

## Award the RFQ

Open the vendor actions menu for the quote that should be awarded.

![Award vendor actions menu](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-43.jpeg)

Select **Award**.

![Award action](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-44.jpeg)

Choose the vendor and review or enter the negotiation amount.

![Award RFQ modal](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-45.jpeg)

Select **Award** to confirm.

![Confirm award action](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-47.jpeg)

Confirm the RFQ status changes to **Awarded** and the financial summary shows the awarded amount and vendor.

![Awarded RFQ status](/help/scribe/publish-award-rfq/scribe-assets/step-48.jpeg)

## Quick Checklist

- RFQ details are reviewed before publishing.
- Vendor recipients and contact emails are correct.
- Quote amount, lead time, adjustments, and quote documents are recorded for each vendor.
- Quotes are compared before award.
- Vendor email recipients, subject, body, and attached RFQ PDF are reviewed before sending.
- Awarded vendor and negotiation amount are confirmed before selecting **Award**.
- RFQ status changes to **Awarded** after completion.
