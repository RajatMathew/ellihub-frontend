# Assign an Employee to a Department

Use this guide as a reference for assigning an existing employee to a department. The example screenshots show sample records only; choose the department and employee that match the organizational update you need to make.

## Open the Department

Navigate to **HR > Departments**.

![Departments list](/help/scribe/assign-employee-to-department/scribe-assets/step-01.jpeg)

Select the department that should receive the employee assignment.

![Department selected](/help/scribe/assign-employee-to-department/scribe-assets/step-02.jpeg)

## Start the Assignment

Select **Assign Employee** from the department profile.

![Assign Employee action](/help/scribe/assign-employee-to-department/scribe-assets/step-03.jpeg)

Use the employee search field to find the employee who should be added to the department.

![Employee search field](/help/scribe/assign-employee-to-department/scribe-assets/step-04.jpeg)

Enter enough of the employee name to narrow the results.

## Select the Employee

Choose the correct employee from the search results.

![Employee selected](/help/scribe/assign-employee-to-department/scribe-assets/step-06.jpeg)

## Confirm the Assignment

Select **Assign to Department** after reviewing the selected employee.

![Assign to Department action](/help/scribe/assign-employee-to-department/scribe-assets/step-07.jpeg)

## Quick Checklist

- The selected department is the correct team or business function.
- The employee search result matches the employee who should be assigned.
- **Assign to Department** is selected after reviewing the employee.
- The department profile reflects the updated employee assignment after saving.
