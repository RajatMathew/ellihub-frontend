# Create a New Contact

Use **Add Contact** when a project needs a new team member, client contact, or stakeholder in the contacts directory. Contacts should include enough detail for project communication, ownership, and reporting.

## Open Contacts

1. Open the ElliHub Finance workspace.
2. Go to **Projects** if you are starting from the project portfolio.
3. Select **Contacts** from the left sidebar under **Directory**.

![Projects page before opening Contacts](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-01.png)

![Contacts item in the sidebar](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-02.png)

## Start a New Contact

Select **Add Contact** from the Contacts page.

![Add Contact button](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-03.png)

## Enter the Contact Name

Click the name field and enter the contact's full name.

Use the person's real business name instead of a nickname or temporary value. This keeps search, reports, and email references clean.

![Contact name field](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-04.png)

![Contact name entered](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-05.png)

## Select a Role

Choose the role that best describes the contact's responsibility, such as **Project Manager**.

![Role selection](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-06.png)

## Add Email

Click the email field and enter the contact's work email address.

Use a valid email address whenever possible. Email is used for communication, lookup, and future workflow references.

![Email field](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-07.png)

![Email entered](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-08.png)

## Add Phone

Click the phone field and enter the contact's phone number.

![Phone field](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-09.png)

![Phone number entered](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-10.png)

## Add Tags or Notes

Use the tags field for short identifiers such as department, decision-maker status, finance ownership, or communication context.

1. Click the tag field.
2. Type the tag.
3. Press **Enter** to add it.

![Tags field](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-11.png)

![Tags field active](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-12.png)

![Tag entered](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-13.png)

## Create the Contact

Review the contact details, then select **Create Contact**.

![Create Contact button](/help/scribe/add-new-contact-to-project/scribe-assets/step-14.png)

## Quick Checklist

- Name uses the contact's real full name.
- Role matches the contact's responsibility.
- Email address is valid.
- Phone number is entered when available.
- Tags describe the contact clearly.
- **Create Contact** is selected after review.
