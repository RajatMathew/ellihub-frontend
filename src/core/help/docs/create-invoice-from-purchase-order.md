# Create an Invoice From a Purchase Order

Use this guide when a vendor invoice needs to be recorded against an existing purchase order. The screenshots show sample invoice numbers, purchase orders, amounts, tax, notes, and files only; use the actual vendor invoice details and backup documents for the project.

## Start a New Invoice

Open the project's **Invoices** page.

![Project Invoices page](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-01.jpeg)

Select **Add Invoice**.

![Add Invoice action](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-02.jpeg)

## Link the Purchase Order

Open the purchase order selector.

![Purchase order selector](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-03.jpeg)

Choose the purchase order that the invoice should be applied against. The vendor is populated from the selected PO.

![Purchase order selected](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-04.jpeg)

## Enter Invoice Details

Enter the invoice number from the vendor invoice.

![Invoice number field](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-05.jpeg)

Set the invoice date and due date.

![Invoice date field](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-07.jpeg)

Add notes when the invoice needs extra context, such as billing period, work completed, or backup reference.

![Invoice notes field](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-08.jpeg)

## Enter Amounts and Tax

Enter the invoice amount before tax.

![Invoice amount field](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-10.jpeg)

Enter the tax amount or tax percentage required for the invoice.

![Tax field](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-13.jpeg)

Review the calculated total before creating the invoice.

![Invoice total review](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-15.jpeg)

## Attach Invoice Documents

Use the document upload area to attach the vendor invoice, backup, receipt, or supporting file.

![Invoice document upload area](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-16.jpeg)

Confirm the selected file appears before creating the invoice.

![Invoice document selected](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-17.jpeg)

## Create and Verify the Invoice

Select **Create Invoice** after the purchase order, invoice details, amount, tax, and documents have been reviewed.

![Create Invoice action](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-18.jpeg)

Confirm the invoice detail page opens with the invoice number, amount, tax, total, due date, and payment status. Newly created invoices may show **Unpaid** until payment is recorded.

![Created invoice detail page](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-19.jpeg)

Verify the payment status.

![Invoice payment status](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-20.jpeg)

Confirm the invoice keeps the uploaded document and links back to the correct purchase order.

![Linked purchase order on invoice](/help/scribe/create-invoice-from-purchase-order/scribe-assets/step-21.jpeg)

## Quick Checklist

- Correct project and **Invoices** page are open.
- Invoice is linked to the correct purchase order.
- Vendor information matches the selected purchase order.
- Invoice number, invoice date, and due date are correct.
- Notes explain any needed billing context.
- Invoice amount, tax, and total are reviewed before creation.
- Vendor invoice or backup documents are attached.
- Created invoice opens with the expected invoice number and unpaid status.
- Linked purchase order card points back to the correct PO.
