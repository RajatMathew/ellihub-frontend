# Create a New Department

Use this guide as a reference for adding a department to the HR area. The example screenshots show sample department details only; enter the department name and description that match your organization.

## Open Departments

Navigate to **HR > Departments**.

![Departments list](/help/scribe/create-new-department/scribe-assets/step-01.jpeg)

Select **Add Department**.

![Add Department action](/help/scribe/create-new-department/scribe-assets/step-02.jpeg)

## Enter Department Details

Use the department name field for the team, division, or business function name.

![Department name field](/help/scribe/create-new-department/scribe-assets/step-03.jpeg)

Enter the department name.

![Department name entered](/help/scribe/create-new-department/scribe-assets/step-04.jpeg)

Use the description field to summarize the department's responsibilities.

![Department description field](/help/scribe/create-new-department/scribe-assets/step-05.jpeg)

Enter a clear department description.

![Department description entered](/help/scribe/create-new-department/scribe-assets/step-06.jpeg)

## Create the Department

Select **Create Department** after reviewing the department details.

![Create Department action](/help/scribe/create-new-department/scribe-assets/step-07.jpeg)

After saving, confirm the department appears in the departments list.

![Department created](/help/scribe/create-new-department/scribe-assets/step-08.jpeg)

## Quick Checklist

- The department name matches the team or business function.
- The description explains the department's responsibilities.
- **Create Department** is selected after reviewing the details.
- The new department appears in the departments list after saving.
