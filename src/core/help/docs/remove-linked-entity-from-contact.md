# Remove a Linked Entity from a Contact

Use this workflow when a contact should no longer be linked to a vendor or general contractor. Removing the linked entity keeps the contact record accurate without deleting the contact itself.

## Find the Contact

Navigate to **Directory > Contacts** and search for the contact you need to update.

![Contacts search results](/help/scribe/remove-linked-entity-from-contact/scribe-assets/step-01.png)

Select the contact from the results.

![Contact selected from search results](/help/scribe/remove-linked-entity-from-contact/scribe-assets/step-02.png)

## Open the Edit Form

Select **Edit Contact** from the contact detail page.

![Edit Contact action](/help/scribe/remove-linked-entity-from-contact/scribe-assets/step-03.png)

## Remove the Linked Entity

In the association section, select the remove icon beside the linked entity.

![Remove linked entity icon](/help/scribe/remove-linked-entity-from-contact/scribe-assets/step-04.png)

Confirm by selecting **Remove**.

![Remove confirmation action](/help/scribe/remove-linked-entity-from-contact/scribe-assets/step-05.png)

## Save the Contact

Select **Save Changes** to apply the update.

![Save Changes action](/help/scribe/remove-linked-entity-from-contact/scribe-assets/step-06.png)

After saving, confirm the contact shows **No entity linked**.

![No entity linked state](/help/scribe/remove-linked-entity-from-contact/scribe-assets/step-07.png)

## Quick Checklist

- The selected contact is the correct person.
- The linked entity is the vendor or general contractor that should be removed.
- **Remove** is selected in the confirmation action.
- **Save Changes** is selected after removing the link.
- The contact shows **No entity linked** after saving.
