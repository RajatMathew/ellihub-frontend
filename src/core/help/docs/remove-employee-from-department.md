# Remove an Employee From a Department

Use this guide as a reference for removing an employee from a department roster. The example screenshots show sample records only; choose the department and employee that match the organizational update you need to make.

## Open the Department Profile

Open the department profile that currently includes the employee assignment.

![Department profile](/help/scribe/remove-employee-from-department/scribe-assets/step-01.jpeg)

## Open Employee Actions

Use the employee row actions menu for the employee you want to remove from the department.

![Employee row actions](/help/scribe/remove-employee-from-department/scribe-assets/step-02.jpeg)

## Remove the Assignment

Select **Remove from Department**.

![Remove from Department action](/help/scribe/remove-employee-from-department/scribe-assets/step-03.jpeg)

## Confirm the Roster

After the update, confirm the department roster reflects the removal.

![Updated department roster](/help/scribe/remove-employee-from-department/scribe-assets/step-04.jpeg)

## Quick Checklist

- The selected department is the correct team or business function.
- The employee row belongs to the employee who should be removed.
- **Remove from Department** is selected from that employee's actions menu.
- The department roster reflects the removal after the update.
