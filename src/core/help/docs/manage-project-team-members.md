# Manage Project Team Members

Use this guide to maintain the team assigned to a project from the project overview. The screenshots show sample employee names only; choose the actual employees and roles for the project.

## Open Team Members

Open the project overview.

![Project overview](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-01.jpeg)

Select the team member avatar or initials in the project header to open the team panel.

![Team member control](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-02.jpeg)

## Add a Team Member

Select **Add Member**.

![Add Member action](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-03.jpeg)

Open the employee selector.

![Employee selector](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-04.jpeg)

Search for and select the employee to add.

![Employee search result](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-06.jpeg)

Confirm the employee appears in the team member list.

![Employee added to team](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-07.jpeg)

## Update a Member Role

Open the role selector for the team member.

![Member role selector](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-08.jpeg)

Choose the role the employee should have on the project, such as **PM**.

![PM role option](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-09.jpeg)

Confirm the updated role appears for the employee.

![PM role selected](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-10.jpeg)

## Transfer Lead PM

Choose **Lead PM** when project leadership should move to another employee.

![Lead PM role option](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-11.jpeg)

Review the transfer confirmation. The current Lead PM becomes a PM when the transfer is completed.

![Lead PM transfer confirmation](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-12.jpeg)

Select **Transfer** to confirm the Lead PM change.

![Transfer action](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-13.jpeg)

## Remove a Team Member

Open the member actions menu.

![Member actions menu](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-14.jpeg)

Select **Remove**.

![Remove action](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-15.jpeg)

Confirm the team list reflects the updated membership.

![Updated team list](/help/scribe/manage-project-team-members/scribe-assets/step-16.jpeg)

## Quick Checklist

- The correct project overview is open.
- The employee being added belongs on the project team.
- The selected role matches the employee's project responsibility.
- Lead PM transfer is reviewed before confirming.
- Removed members no longer appear in the project team list.
